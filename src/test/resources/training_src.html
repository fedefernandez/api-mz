Training Efficiency
To check how well a player is training in an attribute, look at his “training efficiency” meter. The more bars on the meter, the greater the rate of change (green bars represent improvement while red ones indicate a deterioration). When a player has improved enough to reach the next skill level in an attribute, a white ball appears on his meter. When he has deteriorated enough for his attribute skill level to decrease, a red ball appears. Note that each daily change is reflected in a player’s match performances – not just when his number of skill balls changes.

For more information, read the manual.
